REFUND FEE RULE


Percentage(%)deduction of respective standard fee(week from the prescribed Date of Commencement of classes):


Week Zero Week 1st Week 2nd & 3rd Week 4th Week 6th Week
%Fee Deduction (1-Installment) 5% 25% 40% 60% 75%


Note:

  1. Number of weeks for the calculation of fee refund amount will be calculated from the date of commencement of regular classes(Course/Batch)(As mentioned in the academic calendar) till the date which refund of fee is applied for.
  2. The date of commencement of classes will be considered as mentioned in the academic calendar and not from the date on which student joins the institute/attends the classes or deposits the fee even if he/she is a latecomer to the institute.
  3. If a two year program student applies for REFUND of Fee,then the refund will be done as per rules mentioned in the MkWay Guide.Calculation of amount will be done on actual fee of applicable year(i.e. if student applies for refund in class XI then the refund calculation will be done on actual fee of class XI of MkWay Arjun.Similarly,if a student applies for refund in class XII then the refund calculation will be done on class XII only.
  4. All Refund rules will be applicable for academic sessions only
  5. Refund calculation will be done on first installment amount i.e, 60% of total fee till 4th week of batch commencement,after 6th week fee will not be refunded in any case.
  6. Reg.Fee is not refundable
  7. No refund fee will be applicable after 45 days of admission in any case
  8. Full Fees is to be deposited within 45 days of batch commencement
  9. Study material(modules) will be given to students once once in case of any theft or missing of modules student will have to pay 180/-RS. per module to get one new module

An Illustration:-

Assuming a student of Vishesh Course deposits a fee of 1,06,146/-RS i.e after 10% scholarship,as applicable in his case.(Total course fee 1,17,940/-RS. in his case,(Total course fee 1,17,940/-RS. including GST) and he claims refund within the first week itself of commencement of classes, then the refund will be made in this case will bw calculated as under:-

Total Course Fees:                                                   1,17,940/-

Fee Received from the student concerned:        1,06,146/-

Less:Fee Deduction                                                                  

On First Installment i.e. RS 63687 @20%:                  15921/-

Net amount to be refunded:                                      47765/-


The similar,pattern as applicable ,with respect to deduction and refund will be followed for all the courses.



Process for Fee Refund:

If a student want to leave the institute and opt for fee refund he/she should follow under mentioned steps

        1.Collect the pre-formatted application of fee refund from institute fee counter and fill it completely

        2.Submit the filled in refund application along with attachments (Original Fee Receipt(s).Original Identity Card

        3.Collect the acknowledgement reciept of refund application duly signed stamped by institute and keep it for future reference

        4.We issue the fee refund only by online mode by transfer through any NEFT to the Account No. provided in the application form

        5.The fee refund transfer shall be done within 21 working days from the refund application

        6.If the student/parents face any problem in the progress of course fee refund the office manager -Operations/Center Manager can be approached personally/telephonically or through letter/e-mail/fax etc.
