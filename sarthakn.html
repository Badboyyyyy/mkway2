VISHESH Course is specially designed for giving an early advantage to the students currently in 10th class by starting preparation immediately after 10th Board Examination. Ultimate objective of this course is to help students prepare for JEE (Main + Advanced) Examination. Initially, this course begins with the fundamental study for the syllabus and pattern of JEE (Main + Advanced). Students will also be guided to prepare for other competitive exams like KVPY, Olympiads etc
